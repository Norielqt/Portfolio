Our Philosophy

Beauty that honours
the earth & your skin.

We started Lumière with one belief: that what you put on your skin should be as clean, honest, and nourishing as what you put in your body. No compromises. No greenwashing. Just pure, effective botanical science.

What We Stand For

Our core values

✦

Radical Transparency

Every ingredient is listed, explained, and sourced with intention. We publish our full formula breakdowns because we believe you have the right to know exactly what touches your skin.

◎

Clinical Efficacy

Clean beauty doesn't mean weak beauty. Every formula is clinically tested, dermatologist-reviewed, and proven to deliver measurable results — without a single harsh synthetic.

⌾

Planetary Respect

From refillable glass packaging to carbon-offset shipping and zero-waste manufacturing, every decision we make considers the planet as a stakeholder in our business.

The Founder

"I spent ten years as a cosmetic chemist, watching formulas get compromised at every turn — by cost, by trends, by what looked good on a label. Lumière was my way of doing it right."

Sophie Marchand founded Lumière in 2019 after leaving a senior role at a global beauty conglomerate. Armed with a PhD in biochemistry and a deep frustration with industry shortcuts, she developed each formula herself from her laboratory in Lyon — spending an average of 18 months per product before release.

Today, Lumière remains independently owned, refusing outside investment that might compromise its values. Every product is still approved by Sophie before it reaches your hands.

Sophie Marchand
